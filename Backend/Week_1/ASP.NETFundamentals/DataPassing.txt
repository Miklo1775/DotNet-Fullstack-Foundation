Data Passing Techniques in ASP.NET



--Data Passing Techniques
> The best and most popular method to pass data to the view from the controller is through the help of model classes which are normally known as view class.
> With interfaces, we use strongly typed views, its easy to access the object or model which needs to pass the controller.
> Below are the objects we can use to store the value and transfer it from one place to another:
				*ViewData
				*ViewBag
				*TempData
				*Session
				*Cookies
				*QueryString
